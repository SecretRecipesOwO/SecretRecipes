Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta


Back to Home


Ingredients:

200g pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tbsp butter

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Directions:

Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.

Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.

Season with salt and pepper.

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.

Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
