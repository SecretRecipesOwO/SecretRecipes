Chicken Caesar Salad Wraps


Back to Home


Ingredients:

2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded

1/4 cup Caesar dressing

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped

4 large tortillas

Croutons for topping (optional)

Directions:

In a bowl, mix shredded chicken with Caesar dressing and Parmesan cheese.

Lay tortillas flat and distribute the lettuce and chicken mixture evenly.

Add croutons on top if desired.

Roll up each tortilla into a wrap and serve.
