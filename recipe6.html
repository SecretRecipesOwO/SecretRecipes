Spicy Shrimp Tacos


Back to Home


Ingredients:

500g shrimp, peeled and deveined

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp chili powder

1/2 tsp paprika

1/2 tsp cumin

1/2 tsp garlic powder

Salt and pepper to taste

8 small tortillas

1 cup shredded cabbage

1/2 cup diced avocado

Lime wedges for serving

Directions:

In a bowl, mix olive oil, chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss shrimp in the mixture to coat.

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side or until fully cooked.

Warm tortillas and fill with shrimp, shredded cabbage, and diced avocado.

Serve with lime wedges.
