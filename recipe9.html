Mushroom Risotto


Back to Home


Ingredients:

1 cup Arborio rice

2 tbsp olive oil

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

200g mushrooms, sliced

1/2 cup white wine

4 cups chicken or vegetable broth

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tbsp butter

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, saute until softened.

Add mushrooms and cook until they release their juices.

Stir in the Arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes, until lightly toasted.

Pour in white wine and cook until absorbed.

Gradually add broth, one cup at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed. Continue until rice is creamy and tender.

Stir in Parmesan cheese, butter, salt, and pepper. Serve hot.
