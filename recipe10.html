Blueberry Pancakes


Back to Home


Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tbsp sugar

1 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/4 tsp salt

1 egg

1 cup buttermilk

2 tbsp melted butter

1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:

In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

In another bowl, beat the egg and mix in buttermilk and melted butter.

Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Fold in blueberries.

Heat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.

Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Serve with maple syrup.
