Classic Beef Tacos


Back to Home


Ingredients:

500g ground beef

1 small onion, diced

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 packet taco seasoning

8 taco shells

1 cup shredded lettuce

1 cup diced tomatoes

1 cup shredded cheese

Sour cream and salsa for topping

Directions:

In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened.

Stir in taco seasoning and cook according to package instructions.

Warm the taco shells as directed.

Fill each taco shell with ground beef mixture, then top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
