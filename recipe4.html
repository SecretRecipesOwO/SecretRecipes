Vegetable Stir-Fry


Back to Home


Ingredients:

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1 red bell pepper, sliced

1 yellow bell pepper, sliced

1 carrot, julienned

1 zucchini, sliced

1 cup broccoli florets

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp hoisin sauce

1 tsp sesame oil

Cooked rice for serving

Directions:

Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.

Add garlic and saut√© for 30 seconds. Add all vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.

Stir in soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

Serve hot over cooked rice.
